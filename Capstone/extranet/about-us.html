Why We Created Saru Inc. - Educational Technology Books

Saru Inc. was born out of the desire to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving digital world. Our founders, a group of educators and technology enthusiasts, recognized the growing need for educational books that focused on technology, programming, digital tools, and online learning resources. With the rise of the digital age, it became clear that students and professionals alike needed reliable, well-written guides to navigate this new landscape.

At Saru Inc., we specialize in creating educational books that not only teach the fundamentals of technology but also inspire readers to innovate, problem-solve, and think critically. Our mission is to provide learners of all ages with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed in a world that is becoming increasingly driven by technology.

Our Values in Educational Technology

Our books are crafted with the following values in mind:

  • Innovation: We aim to stay ahead of the curve, covering emerging technologies like AI, cloud computing, data science, and more.
  • Clarity: Our content is designed to be accessible and easy to understand, ensuring that complex tech topics are broken down into simple concepts.
  • Practicality: We focus on practical applications of technology, providing real-world examples, exercises, and projects to reinforce learning.
  • Adaptability: Our books are suitable for students, educators, and professionals, offering various learning formats (e.g., e-books, print, online resources).
  • Inspiration: Our goal is not only to teach but to inspire future tech leaders, developers, and innovators.

Our Journey So Far

Founded in 2015, Saru Inc. started with a single book on basic programming concepts. As the demand for technology education grew, so did our catalog. Today, we offer books covering a range of topics, including web development, machine learning, cybersecurity, digital marketing, and more. We have helped thousands of students, educators, and professionals harness the power of technology to advance their careers and personal growth.

We continue to expand our offerings, with plans to release new books on cutting-edge topics like blockchain, Internet of Things (IoT), and artificial intelligence. Our vision is to create a world where every learner, regardless of background, can access the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in a digital world.
