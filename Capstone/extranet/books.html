History of Saru Inc. Books

Saru Inc. was founded with a mission to provide quality educational books for learners of all ages. Since our establishment, we have worked tirelessly to curate a collection of books that cater to various subjects and skill levels, from early childhood education to advanced university textbooks.

Our journey started in 2000 with a small selection of math and science books. Over the years, we expanded our collection to technology, and more, becoming a trusted name for students, teachers, and educational institutions worldwide.

We take pride in offering books that are not only educational but also engaging, helping students unlock their full potential in their academic and personal growth.
